The Rules

  • Player 1 always goes first.
  • The game ends after 10 rounds.
  • There are 3 questions per round.
  • After each set, a prompt will appear.
  • Correct Answers will be highlighted in green.
  • Incorrect Answers will be highlighted in red.
  • A round ends after all 3 round questions have been answered correctly.
  • The game ends after all 30 game questions have been answered correctly.
  • The game can be reset anytime after a question is answered correctly.
